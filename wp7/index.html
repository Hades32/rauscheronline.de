Opera Link for WP7

This tool gives you full access to your Bookmarks, Notes and SpeedDials from your Opera browser (Mini, Mobile and Desktop)!

To be able to use this tool you have to enable Opera Link in Opera. For Opera Mobile and Opera Mini you can find this under options. On the desktop click the "O" menu and choose synchronize Opera.

More information and feedback (or donations) at my.opera.com/Hades32
